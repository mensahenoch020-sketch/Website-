Residential Appraisals

Our residential appraisal services provide accurate, heavily researched valuations for single-family homes, townhouses, and condominiums across the Tallahassee area. We utilize the Uniform Residential Appraisal Report (URAR) and the latest market data to ensure complete accuracy.

Common reasons for residential appraisals:

  • Primary Mortgage Financing & Refinancing
  • Pre-Listing Appraisals for Sellers
  • PMI (Private Mortgage Insurance) Removal
  • Divorce Settlement & Asset Division

Estate & Probate Appraisals

Settling an estate requires a highly specific type of property valuation. Often, you will need a "retrospective appraisal" that determines the fair market value of a property based on a prior date (such as a date of death), rather than the current date.

Our Estate Services Include:

  • Date of Death Valuations for IRS / Tax Purposes
  • Step-Up in Basis Determinations
  • Trust Planning & Asset Management
  • IRS-Compliant Narrative Reports

We work directly with executors, CPAs, and estate attorneys to provide seamless, confidential service.
